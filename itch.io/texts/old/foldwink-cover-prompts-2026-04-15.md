# Foldwink — Cover & Marketing Prompt Pack

**Date:** 2026-04-15
**Companion:** `foldwink-art-brief-2026-04-15.md`
**For:** Gemini / Imagen / Midjourney / DALL·E / SDXL — prompts are
generator-agnostic.

All prompts assume the Foldwink palette:

```
background #0f1115   tile body #1a1e26   text #e9edf2
accent (Wink) #7cc4ff
solved groups #f5c86b  #8cd28e  #ef9e9e  #b49cf0
```

Negative-prompt base list (reuse everywhere, add per-section
specifics):

> no 3D, no isometric blocks, no bokeh, no lens flare, no neon gradients,
> no cartoon mascots, no stock puzzle clichés (jigsaw, lightbulb,
> crossword, Sudoku grid), no photorealism, no chromatic aberration,
> no grain, no noise textures, no watermarks, no borders, no frames,
> no UI chrome, no browser chrome, no Unicode box drawing, no glitch
> effects, no low-poly geometry, no cel-shading, no hand-drawn textures,
> no clutter, no text crowding.

## 1. Main cover image (630 × 500, itch listing)

### 1A — Primary

> A clean editorial-minimalist promotional image for a browser puzzle
> game called **Foldwink**. Near-black background `#0f1115`. Centered
> composition: a tight 4×4 grid of rounded dark tiles (`#1a1e26`,
> corner radius ~14px, subtle 1px inner highlight), sixteen tiles total,
> arranged with small uniform gaps. Four tiles already solved in four
> soft desaturated pastels — amber `#f5c86b`, sage `#8cd28e`, coral
> `#ef9e9e`, lavender `#b49cf0` — forming one color per row. Above the
> grid, a row of four rounded pill tabs with uppercase letters: three
> show partial hints like `F··`, `P··`, `H····`, and one is fully
> revealed reading **BALL** in accent blue `#7cc4ff` with a small
> 4-point sparkle glyph. Wordmark **Foldwink** at top in bold geometric
> sans-serif, `#e9edf2`, letter-spacing tight. No other text.
> Generous negative space. Flat 2D vector look, no depth, no shadows
> beyond a hairline. Style: Vercel / Linear / Are.na minimal. Aspect
> 630×500.

### 1B — Restrained / premium variant

> Identical layout to 1A, but the grid is only partially visible —
> bottom-right quadrant cropped out of frame, suggesting more content.
> Only one tab pill visible, fully winked to `BALL` in `#7cc4ff`. All
> other tiles are monochrome `#1a1e26` except a single solved cell in
> `#b49cf0` on the top row. Wordmark bottom-left, tagline in `#8b93a3`
> directly below reading _"Daily grouping puzzle"_. Feels like a
> premium indie publication. Editorial rhythm. Aspect 630×500.

### 1C — Punchy / storefront variant

> Same palette and grid, but bolder: four fully solved groups visible
> (amber top row, sage second, coral third, lavender bottom), each
> group connected by a subtle matching tint. Tab row above the grid
> shows one fully-revealed category **BALL** in `#7cc4ff` with a bright
> sparkle. Wordmark **Foldwink** large, top-center, with a single
> stroke underline in accent blue. One-line tagline beneath:
> _Find 4 hidden groups of 4_. High contrast, instantly legible at
> 200px. Aspect 630×500.

### Composition notes

- Grid centered on a 16:12.5 canvas, 60% of height.
- Wordmark upper area, 10% of height.
- No element within 24px of any edge.
- Tab row above grid uses letter-spacing 0.18em.

### Text placement

- Wordmark: top third, horizontally centered (1A, 1C) or bottom-left
  (1B).
- Avoid more than 6 total words anywhere on the image.

### Negative (additions)

> no trophies, no confetti, no crown icons, no app-store gloss,
> no radial lighting, no vignettes.

## 5. Screenshot enhancement prompts (for overlay passes)

These prompts are for _retouching around_ existing captured screenshots,
not replacing them. Run on a copy, never on the raw capture.

### 5A — Adding a wide margin with tagline

> Around a centered 1440×900 screenshot of a dark web puzzle UI, add a
> 240px border of uniform `#0f1115`. In the top-left of that margin,
> place the wordmark **Foldwink** in bold geometric sans `#e9edf2`. In
> the top-right, a short tagline _Daily grouping puzzle_ in muted
> `#8b93a3`. Do not alter the embedded screenshot itself. Do not crop
> or rescale inside the frame. Keep all borders crisp, no gradients.

### 5B — Creating a pair-up device/desktop mockup

> Place a 1440×900 desktop screenshot of the Foldwink menu on the
> left and a 390×844 mobile screenshot of the Foldwink medium-wink
> state on the right, both floating over a `#0f1115` canvas. Do not
> render physical device frames or bezels — just the raw screenshots
> with a hairline 1px `#2e343f` inner border. Align vertical centers.
> Generous gap between them. Wordmark **Foldwink** top-center in
> `#e9edf2`. No drop shadows, no reflections.

### 5C — Social quote card

> A social-format 1200×1200 square. Center: a single 4×4 grid
> screenshot of Foldwink with one group solved in `#8cd28e`. Below:
> one line of review-style text in `#e9edf2`, _"A calm puzzle for
> thinking people"_, attribution in `#8b93a3` below. Wordmark tiny
> top-center. No speech bubble, no quote marks rendered as glyphs — just
> italic type.

## Style tokens to reuse verbatim across prompts

Drop any of these at the end of a prompt to lock consistency:

- `flat 2D, no depth, no 3D, editorial minimalism`
- `Vercel / Linear / Are.na aesthetic`
- `letter-spacing 0.18em on small caps`
- `rounded-square corner radius ~14px`
- `pastel solves, not primaries; accent is #7cc4ff only`
- `dark canvas #0f1115 dominant, negative space is a feature`

## Primary visual direction (recommendation)

**Lead with the winked-tab grid composition.** Variant **1A** for
itch.io cover; variant **3A** for the press/hero banner; variant **2B**
(brand mark only) for any square social avatar or profile image. This
keeps the art instantly identifiable as _Foldwink specifically_ and
communicates the Wink mechanic without a single explanatory word.

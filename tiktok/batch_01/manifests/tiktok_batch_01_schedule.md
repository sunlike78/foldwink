# Foldwink — TikTok Batch 01 Posting Schedule

**Horizon:** 8 days (Day 1 → Day 8) with 3 reserves available on demand.
**Cadence target:** 1–2 posts per day, never more than 2 to avoid algorithmic cannibalisation.
**Time zone:** use viewer local time if you know it; otherwise US Eastern (where English puzzle-game audience sits heaviest).
**Why 8 days, not 14:** we want signal early. If the strongest hook is clear by Day 3, stop following the schedule and double down.

## Interpretation rules — what to watch in 24 h

Don’t rush the read. TikTok hourly metrics are noisy for new accounts.

1. **First 6 h — completion rate.** If completion <40 %, the hook is landing late. Trim the first half-second in the next batch, not this one.
2. **First 24 h — comments/saves ratio.** Clips 02, 03, 05 are written to earn comments. If saves > comments, audience is watching but not engaging — re-pin with a spicier first-comment in hour 24.
3. **48 h — profile clicks.** Clip 10 is the main profile-click driver. If it outperforms challenge clips 3-to-1 on profile clicks but not on views, the right batch-02 move is **more curiosity clips, fewer challenge clips**.
4. **Do not boost paid.** Let each clip breathe organically for 72 h before any paid push. Paid boost on cold clips teaches the algorithm the wrong audience.

## Replacement rules — when to swap in reserves

Swap a main clip for a reserve if **any** of these fire in the first 48 h:

| Symptom | Swap in | Swap out |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Clip 01 completion <35 % | r1 · One Move Left | retire 01 |
| Clip 03 comments <5 | r2 · Looks Easy Alt | retire 03 |
| Clip 02 profile-clicks low | r3 · Can You Solve Alt | retire 02 |

Otherwise, hold reserves until Batch 02.

---

## What to stop doing mid-batch

If Day 3 data shows curiosity clips (10, future siblings) outperform challenge clips 2-to-1 on profile clicks, **skip clips 04 and 09** on Days 3 and 8 and replace them with quick recuts of clip 10’s structure with different puzzles. The point of Batch 01 is signal, not completion of the schedule.

## Audio note

Every clip ships with tactile SFX only — no licensed music. This is intentional:

- business accounts on TikTok have limited access to the commercial music library, and licensing risk on new brand content is not worth the upside
- all 13 clips are **post-ready as-is** — do not add trending sounds on top, it muddies the SFX rhythm that carries the satisfying beats

If a specific clip demands music for a later test, use TikTok’s royalty-free library tags `relaxing` or `clean beats` — never top-40.

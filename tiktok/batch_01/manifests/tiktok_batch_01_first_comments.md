# Foldwink — TikTok Batch 01 First-Comment Suggestions

Post the **first comment from the brand account itself** within 30 s of publishing. Pin it. This is the highest-leverage move on TikTok: it seeds the comment pattern other viewers copy.

Rules:

1. Do **not** post a promo comment ("check out our game"). TikTok down-weights those.
2. Do **not** tag the link. If you must, tag it in the bio line instead.
3. Aim for a comment that is worth replying to — a question, a confession, a side-note.
4. If a clip has <5 comments after 12 h, **swap** the pinned first comment for a spicier alternate.

Primary comment = post this immediately. Alt = swap to after 12 h if cold.

## Reply cadence — first 24 h

When comments arrive:

- reply within 15 min during primetime (18:00–22:00 local)
- reply within 60 min off-peak
- never copy-paste — each reply visibly different
- if someone posts a time in your `drop your time` thread, **reply with your own time**, not just a ❤️
- if someone asks "how do I play" — drop the bio link once per thread, no more

Do not reply to trolls. Don’t hide them either. Let them sit.
